Mitigation Techniques

Hazard: Inadequate maintenance

Task: Crane Operation

Mitigation Techniques:

1926.501(b)(5) - Formwork and reinforcing steel. Each employee on the face of formwork or reinforcing steel shall be protected from falling 6 feet (1.8 m) or more to lower levels by personal fall arrest systems, safety net systems, or positioning device systems.

Personal fall arresting system: Personal fall arresting system: Personal fall arresting systems and their use shall comply with the following provisions. 1926.502(d)

Safety net system: Safety net system: safety net systems and their use shall comply with the following provisions. 1926.502(c)

Positioning Device system: Positioning device systems and their use shall conform to the following provisions. 1926.502(e)
[Source from: Occupational Safety and Health Administration(OSHA)]
