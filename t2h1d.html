Manual Handling

Meaning: Manual handling refers to the lifting, carrying, pushing, or pulling of heavy objects or materials manually, without the assistance of mechanical aids.

Consequences: When workers engage in manual handling tasks without proper lifting techniques or assistance, they are at risk of being crushed or trapped under heavy objects, such as steel bars or reinforcement materials. This can result in serious injuries, including fractures, internal injuries, or even fatalities, if the weight of the object overwhelms the worker's ability to support it.
