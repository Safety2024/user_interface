Struck by steel bars

Meaning: This hazard occurs when workers are struck by steel bars or reinforcement materials, typically due to being in close proximity to swinging or falling objects or during the handling and positioning of steel bars.



Consequences: Striking or being struck by steel bars can cause cuts, lacerations, or puncture wounds to the body, especially if workers are not wearing appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) such as gloves, safety glasses, or hard hats.
