Mitigation Techniques

Hazard: Fall from height

Task: Reinforcement and Steel Erection

Mitigation Techniques:

For Reinforcement: 1926.501(b)(5) - Formwork and reinforcing steel. Each employee on the face of formwork or reinforcing steel shall be protected from falling 6 feet (1.8 m) or more to lower levels by personal fall arrest systems, safety net systems, or positioning device systems.
For steel: 1926.760(a)(1) - Except as provided by paragraph (a)(3) of this section, each employee engaged in a steel erection activity who is on a walking/working surface with an unprotected side or edge more than 15 feet (4.6 m) above a lower level shall be protected from fall hazards by guardrail systems, safety net systems, personal fall arrest systems, positioning device systems or fall restraint systems.
[Source: Occupational Safety and Health Administration(OSHA)]
