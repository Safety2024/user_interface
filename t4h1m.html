Mitigation Techniques

Hazard: Collapse of formwork

Task: Formwork and Shuttering

Mitigation Techniques:

Formwork is made from different materials and requires great skill and experience in its manufacture. To produce concrete forms that meet all job requirements, the construction engineer must understand the characteristics, properties, and behaviors of the materials used, be able to estimate the loads applied to the forms, and be familiar with the advantages and shortcomings of various forming systems.
