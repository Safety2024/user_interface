Fall from height

Meaning:This hazard pertains to the risk of workers falling from elevated surfaces, such as formwork platforms or scaffolding, while performing formwork and shuttering tasks at height. Especially when doing edge construction at height such as : beam ,column, slab of edge of the building.

Consequences:: Falls from height can result in serious injuries or fatalities, particularly if workers land on hard surfaces or structures below. The impact of a fall from height can cause spinal cord injuries, fractures, and traumatic brain injuries, with potentially devastating consequences for the affected worker's health and well-being.
